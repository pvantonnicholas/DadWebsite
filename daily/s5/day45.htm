Reading for Day 45

The Nicholas Family Had A Very Relaxing Holiday Near The Connemara Mountains, County Galway, Ireland.

"I lift up my eyes to the hills — where does my help come from? My help comes from the LORD, the Maker of heaven and earth."

Psalm 121:1-2

"How beautiful on the mountains are the feet of those who bring good news."

Isaiah 52:7

We hope the photographs will speak for themselves. The cosy and comfortable hotel we stayed in was majestically located on the shores of the west coast of Ireland, surrounded between the Twelve Bens mountains and the vast Atlantic Ocean. Outside our windows the view moulded neatly for us to enhance our faith — we could see the Connemara loop. The perfect natural surroundings for us to meditate on the Word of God. There are beautiful and historical sites to visit. The Connemara National Park, Kylemore Abbey, Killary Fjord and various other archaeological sites.
