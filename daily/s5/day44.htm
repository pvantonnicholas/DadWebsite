This Is Our Beautiful Back Garden. Well Tendered!

The LORD will comfort Israel again and make her deserts blossom. Her barren wilderness will become as beautiful as Eden — the garden of the LORD.

Isaiah 51:3

Valerie Meilton BSc, State Registered Dietitian is our residential gardener.

On Friday, 16th January 1999, we were in Valerie’s house in Claygate, Surrey. This was the day her Mum spoke her final words to us before she departed 6 days later. “Please take care of my only daughter”. We have since adopted her as a member of our family. To read further press LINK:

May the Blessing of the garden of Eden come upon the Nicholas Family.

This page features beautiful photos of the well-tended back garden, including lilies, rhododendrons, pansies, flaming silver plants, and various flowers, symbolising the restoration and beauty described in Isaiah 51:3.

 

We want to acknowledge our Lord's blessings on this website. It was blessed and inaugurated officially on 1 August 2002.
