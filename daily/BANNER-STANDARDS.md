# Daily Readings Banner Image Standards

**Last Updated:** 2026

## Target Specification (New Standard from s6/s7 onwards)

- **Resolution:** 1920 × 960 pixels
- **Aspect Ratio:** 2:1 (exactly)
- **Format:** JPEG
- **Quality:** 85–90
- **Color Profile:** sRGB

**Rationale:**
- Good balance of width for impact while remaining practical.
- Works excellently with `object-fit: cover` at the current 400px banner height.
- Easier to compose than the very wide 3:1 images used in some earlier sections.
- 1920px width is a safe web standard.

**File Naming Convention (already in use):**
- Per-day banners: `day51-banner.jpg`, `day52-banner.jpg`, etc.
- Section hero banners: `banner.jpg` or `banner.png` (in the sX folder)

## Visual Style Guidance

- Photographic / atmospheric / cinematic realism preferred.
- Spiritual, contemplative, or meaningful mood.
- Natural or dramatic lighting.
- Good negative space or calm areas in the lower third (important for dark gradient overlays used in section indexes).
- Mix of landscape, symbolic, architectural, and portrait styles is acceptable and encouraged.
- Avoid overly saturated, cartoonish, or heavily stylized AI looks unless deliberately chosen for a specific day.

## Generation Notes

- Use Grok Imagine with aspect ratio close to 2:1.
- Generate at high resolution, then crop/resize to 1920x960 if needed.
- Each banner should feel connected to the specific day's theme and title.
