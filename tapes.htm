The Abiding Presence of God:

The Lord's prayer bore fruit. The testimonies are inserted in the tapes. The covenant keeping God and His powerful names; El-Shaddai, God El-Elohim, Jehovah Jireh, Jehovah Shammah and the other precious names were faithful to their promises.

This is the message Anton takes to the nations of the world to pray and pray and pray!

God is opening up prayer meetings in various parts of the world that host 24-hours a day meetings, interpersed with intense worship and prophetic prayer. Our prophetic prayer is that all of us will join in these 24-hour prayer meetings and for God the Holy Ghost to protect our family members from all forms of dangers, natural and spiritual.
